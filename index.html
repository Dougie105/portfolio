About Me

I am origionally from the midwest/east coast (Ohio, FLorida, Georgia, Virginia, Indiana). I spent 5 years in the United States Coast Gurad, 4 years as a quartermaster and helmsman and 1 year as Mechanic. I got out of the military and did a variety of jobs and eventualy landed as a maintenance technician at a small recycling company. I knew that maintiance wasnt going to pay me enough in the long run to be able to afford to live and have children. Thats when I started looking into software development, and I instantly fell in love (with the wages and the challenges). Software development has challenged me in ways that no manual labor position ever could and I am greatful I found this path and can share my passion for development with you as well.
